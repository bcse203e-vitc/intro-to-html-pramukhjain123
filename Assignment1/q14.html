Stock Market Definitions

Stock
A type of security that signifies ownership in a corporation and represents a claim on part of the corporation's assets and earnings.
Dividend
A portion of a company's earnings that is paid to shareholders, typically on a regular basis.
Bear Market
A market condition where prices are falling, encouraging selling.
Bull Market
A market condition where prices are rising, encouraging buying.
Portfolio
A collection of financial investments like stocks, bonds, commodities, and cash.
IPO
Initial Public Offering, the process by which a private company becomes publicly traded by offering its shares to the public for the first time.
Market Capitalization
The total market value of a company's outstanding shares, calculated as share price multiplied by the number of shares.
